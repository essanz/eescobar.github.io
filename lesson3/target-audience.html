Target Audience

This site targets residents from the towns of Preston, Soda Springs, and Fish Haven, who need to have up-to-date weather information for their day-to-day activities. They are usually 21-years or older, who have completed High School, and are of any income level. They have some experience with weather related topics since they use this information to plan their activities.


Personas

Persona #1.

  • Persona: Farmer.
  • Fictional Name: Steve Lowe.
  • Main Responsibilities: Farm Owner.
  • Demographics: 50 years old, married, father of 4 children, has a High School diploma.
  • Goals and Tasks: Is focused on developing new ways to increase his annual harvest to close more business deals with his customers. His day usually start before sunrise and depends on weather reports to plan for the day.
  • Environment: He is responsible for the day-to-day operations of the farm. Has more than 35 years of experience as a farmer, and almost 15 as a farm owner.
  • Quote: "How can we do things better."

Persona #2.

  • Persona: Stay-at-home mother.
  • Fictional Name: Kathy Welch.
  • Main Responsibilities: Stay-at-home mother and amateur runner.
  • Demographics: 40 years old, married, mother of 2 children, has a bachelor's degree.
  • Goals and Tasks: Her main focus is on raising her two children. Enjoys planning outdoor activities with them. She is also interested in improving her health and has chosen running as a physical activity.
  • Environment: Spends most of her time with her children, when they are not in school. Enjoys running with friends or alone in the mornings.
  • Quote: "Outdoor activities help me relax."

Scenarios

  1. Will it rain tomorrow so I can plan on whether I need to water the garden?
  2. I am planning on taking my children outside to play this afternoon. What will be the UV index?
  3. Will this coming weekend be a good one to go hiking?
  4. Will I be able to host an party outdoors this Friday?
  5. What type of clothing will I need to go running tomorrow morning?
