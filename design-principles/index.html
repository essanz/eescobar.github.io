Design Principles

I. Enrique Escobar

Volkswagen

vw.com

Fitt's Law. The website makes it easier for users to tap on links by making them look isolated from paragraphs or other links.

Walmart

walmart.com

Hick's Law. The user is guided to the most important or newest sections. Other sections that are considered less relevant seem to occupy a second place.

The Church of Jesus Christ of Latter-Day Saints

churchofjesuschrist.org

Whitesepace and Clean Design. The design of this webpage is clean. Each section has an image and a very decriptive title that is easy to read and catches the attention of the user.
